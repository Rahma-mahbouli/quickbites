# QuikBites - Food Delivery Service

## Overview

QuikBites is a cutting-edge food delivery service designed to meet the demands of modern life. With our user-friendly online platform and mobile app, customers can easily order delicious meals from their favorite restaurants and have them delivered right to their doorstep.

## Features

- **Extensive Restaurant Selection:** QuikBites provides a diverse range of restaurant options, ensuring that there's something for every palate. From local gems to popular chains, our platform offers a variety of culinary choices.

- **Intuitive Ordering Process:** Ordering food with QuikBites is a breeze. Users can effortlessly browse restaurant menus, customize their orders, and complete the transaction with just a few clicks.

- **Swift and Reliable Delivery:** We prioritize efficiency and reliability in our delivery services. Once an order is placed, our dedicated delivery personnel ensure that your food reaches you promptly.

- **Real-Time Order Tracking:** Stay in the loop with our real-time order tracking feature. Know exactly where your food is in the delivery process and receive timely updates.

## How to Use

1. **Sign Up or Log In:** Create a QuikBites account or log in if you're an existing user.

2. **Enter Delivery Location:** Provide your delivery address to see which restaurants are available in your area.

3. **Browse Restaurants:** Explore the diverse range of restaurants on QuikBites. Check out their menus and discover new culinary delights.

4. **Place Your Order:** Select your desired dishes, customize your order if needed, and proceed to checkout.

5. **Secure Payment:** Choose from a variety of secure payment options to complete your transaction.

6. **Track Your Order:** Use the real-time tracking feature to monitor the status of your order as it makes its way to you.

7. **Enjoy Your Meal:** Once your order arrives, sit back, relax, and savor the delightful flavors brought to you by QuikBites.
